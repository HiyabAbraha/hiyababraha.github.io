Also! The Albuquerque Balloon Fiesta is coming up!


The festival is so much fun and it takes place Sunday October 2nd to Sunday October 10th this year.

It's a perfect family-friendly event and the food is legendary. But before you go, make sure to:

  • Bring jackets—it gets pretty cold at Balloon Fiesta Park early in the morning
  • Bring money—the breakfast burritos at the Park are among the best in the city
  • Cameras—you will definitely want to take photos as the balloons takeoff
  • Lawn chairs—before takeoff, you'll be waiting around for a bit and might want a place to sit


You can buy your tickets here and I hope to see some of you there!
