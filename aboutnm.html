State Fun Facts!

Our state bird is the roadrunner

The first nuclear bomb ever detonated was dropped in Alamogordo, NM in 1945

New Mexico has more livestock than people...seriously

We were the first state to adopt an official question: “Red or Green”, referring to hatch chile

The Palace of Governors, established in 1610, is the oldest government building in the United States
