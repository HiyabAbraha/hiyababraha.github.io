Albuquerque

City Fun Facts!

City founded in 1706

Microsoft was founded on April 4, 1975 in Albuquerque

We have a charity dedicated to the preservation of prairie dogs called Prairie Dog Pals

All three High School Musical movies take place in ABQ (So does Breaking Bad)

The soil in Albuquerque is not suitable for high-rise buildings (ABQ is located in the Rio Grande River Valley) so the tallest building in the city is only 22 stories or 351 feet tall.
